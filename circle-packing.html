Circle Packing

D3 v5

SFFD Calls for Service Categories, Mar 2020

Encoding

The location of each request is encoded positionally on the map of SF such that each circle represents one 311 request made in the Rec and Parks category. The color of the circle is the source of the request - Open311 app, phone call or web.

Interactivity

Hovering over the SF map will highlight the district and show the district name. Hovering over a circle will provide request details (incl. an image) in a table to the top-left. Dragging the map will allow you to pan accross and zooming using the trackpad or mousewheel will zoom into the map.

Conclusion

From the map, it is seen that a majority of requests are coming from the Open311 mobile app. The number of requests made by phone call looks about the same as that by web.

Large clusters of requests are seen within parks such as Golden Gate Park and Dolores Park, which is no mystery.

A vast majority of requests are closed as opposed to open.
