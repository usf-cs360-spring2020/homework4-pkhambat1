SFFD Calls for Service Categories, Mar 2020

Encoding

The following categories for SFFD Service Calls are encoded as a circular dendogram - All Incidents, Call Type Group, Call Type and Unit Type. The hierarchy level of each node is encoded with color.

Interactivity

Hovering over the circles will display information about the incident grouping and highlight the ancestors. Clicking on a child node will expand its children and clicking on a root node will expand to its parent.

Conclusion

From the dendogram, I can see that there are several ways to categorize service calls attributed to alarms as opposed to calls for potentially life threatening events which only have 2 categories.

Medical Incidents have required a vast variety of unit types.

Non-life threatening service calls outnumber all of the rest combined.
